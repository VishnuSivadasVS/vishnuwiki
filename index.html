Vishnu Sivadas

Building developer tools, SaaS products, and practical software through Codes Easy.

Vishnu (Vishnu Sivadas) is a software developer and entrepreneur focused on developer tools, SaaS platforms, and scalable backend systems.

As founder of Codes Easy, the work focuses on custom development, programming education, and product ideas built around practical developer needs.

Background

Based in Kerala, India, Vishnu has been building software since 2015. The work spans full-stack engineering, DevOps, and product-building across multiple industries. Codes Easy was founded as a vehicle for both client work and self-directed products.

Areas of expertise span developer tooling, SaaS architecture, backend systems, DevOps, and product engineering — with a focus on practical, adoption-friendly software that prioritizes developer experience.

Approach & Philosophy

"I believe in building software that solves real problems — not hypothetical ones. Things should be useful, fast, and easy to adopt."

The development philosophy centers on practical impact over technical sophistication — products built around clean APIs, minimal setup, and clear documentation. This is reflected in tools like DevKit (dev environment) and xpos.dev (zero-config tunneling).
